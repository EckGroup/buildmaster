About BuildMaster Pro

Founded in 2008, BuildMaster Pro has grown from a small local contractor to a leading provider of skilled construction workers across multiple trades. Our mission is to connect clients with the best craftsmen for their projects.

We carefully vet all our workers, ensuring they have the proper certifications, experience, and commitment to quality that our clients deserve. Our team includes union and non-union professionals across all major construction trades.

All workers undergo background checks and skills verification

Comprehensive insurance coverage for all projects

Commitment to safety with OSHA-trained personnel
